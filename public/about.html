Our Story

BUILT ON A BELIEF

Founded in 2025, Prince Automotive Group LLC was born from a simple belief: buying a pre-owned luxury vehicle should be just as prestigious an experience as buying new. We saw an industry full of pressure tactics, hidden fees, and impersonal transactions — and we decided to build something different.

At Prince, every vehicle in our collection is hand-selected, thoroughly inspected, and priced with complete transparency. We don't just sell cars — we curate experiences. Whether you're purchasing your dream vehicle, exploring a flexible lease, or renting a premium car for any occasion, we treat every client like royalty.

Our commitment goes beyond the transaction. We believe in building lasting relationships, offering white-glove concierge service, and ensuring every customer drives away not just satisfied, but genuinely impressed.
